Nagaland
Financial AssistanceNagalandState Research Scholarship
Details
Detailed 
Directorate of Higher Education Nagaland Kohima invites students who are eligible to submit applications for the Nagaland State Research Scholarship. This scholarship is specially designed for students who are pursuing research work. Under the scheme, beneficiaries will get cash awards to conduct their research work and financial aid to complete their studies. 

Benefits
Benefits
Under the Nagaland State Research Scholarship beneficiaries will get financial aid to complete their studies.
This scholarship is specially designed for students who are pursuing research work. Under the scheme, beneficiaries will get cash awards to conduct their research work.
Eligibility
Eligibility 
Applicable for Scheduled Tribe and Indigenous students of Nagaland who are pursuing M.Phil/Ph.D/D.Litt. course from recognized Universities within India.
Scholars who have availed this Scholarship three times are no longer eligible.
Applicant must not be a beneficiary of any other Fellowship/Scholarship scheme.
Applicant must not be an employee of any government/semi-government establishment

Documents Required
Applicants need to upload their Aadhaar card and the front page of Bank Passbook during the online application form filling and attested xerox copies of the following documents with the hard copy of the application:
For fresh application
Class 10 admit card and mark sheet.
Last qualifying final examination Marksheet and Admit Card.
Scheduled Tribe Certificate and Indigenous Certificate issued by the competent designated authority.
Recognition letter of the University (Not necessary for Central University).
Synopsis of the proposed Research Work duly countersigned by the Guide or Supervisor (for Ph.D. scholars).
Registration Card/Letter (for PhD scholars).
Front page of Bank Passbook.
Aadhaar Card.
Admission receipt.

For renewal application
PhD scholars should enclose a copy of the Progress Report of his/her Research work duly attested and signed by the Guide or Supervisor.
MPhil scholars should enclose a certificate from the Guide/Supervisor stating the continuation of his/her MPhil works.
PhD students must submit Registration Card/Letter & Synopsis if not enclosed during fresh application.
Front page of Bank Passbook.
Aadhaar Card.
