Uniform Assistance To SC Students
Gujarat
Financial AssistanceScheduled CasteUniform Assistance
Details
Objective 
Main Objective is to provide economic support and motivation for education. Under this scheme, assistance is given to Students studying in classes 1 to 8. 
Through this scheme, the Gujarat government promotes primary education.

Benefits
Rs.900/- assistance for three pairs of uniforms.
Eligibility
The student should be of Scheduled Caste.
Students should be studying in classes 1 to 8.
No Income Limit.
Application Process
Online
Applicants need to visit the official website https://www.digitalgujarat.gov.in

New applicants have to register his/her self first.
At the time of registration, applicants have to provide their mobile number, email & set password.
Update their profile.
Applying for the scholarship.
Documents Required
Identity proof: Aadhar card, Voter ID, College ID, etc.
Residence proof: PAN card, Residence certificate, etc.
Income proof.
Aadhar card.
Passport-size photograph.
Academics certificates.
Bank details e.g. Account number, IFSC, MICR code.
Other documents may be required.
Caste certificate.
