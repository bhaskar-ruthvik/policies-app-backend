Old Age Home (Sahara)
Bihar
Shelter HomesSocial WelfareVrihad Sahayata
Details
Under this scheme construction and operation of the old age home "Sahara" will be included in which destitute Housing, clothes, food, medical facilities, etc. are made available for qualitative improvement in the standard of living of poor and poor elders, for cooperation and proper care.

Benefits
Construction and operation of old age home "Sahara"
These old age homes also include housing, clothes, food, medical facilities etc. 
Facilities will be available for qualitative improvement in the standard of living, cooperation and proper care of destitute and poor old people of the state.
Eligibility
Applicants should come under the below poverty line ( BPL) category (or)
Annual Income should be below Rs. 60,000/-
Application Process
Offline
Applicants can apply through the application in plain paper to the District Social Security Cell.
The committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Magistrate will approve the proposal. 
Documents Required
Age Proof.
Income proof document.
BPL Card (If applicable).
Two passport-size photographs.
