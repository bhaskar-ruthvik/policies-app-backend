Gaon Ki Beti
Madhya Pradesh
Financial AssistanceHigher EducationScholarship
Details
MP Gaon Ki Beti Yojna scholarship is an initiative to support the education of girl children from rural areas who have passed Class 12 with first division. This scheme aims at the following objectives:
Provide financial assistance to girls in rural areas.
Encourage girls to pursue higher education.

Benefits
Assistance up to Rs. 5000/- is provided annually to the girl student. 
Eligibility
The girl child should have secured 60% or more marks in the 12th examination.
The girl student should be living in a rural area.
Application Process
Online
SC/ST/OBC/General girl students should register themselves on the portal.
Click on New Registration for Gaon Ki Beti and fill out the required details.
Now they can apply for the benefits of the scheme by logging in with the registration ID on the portal.
Documents Required
Class 12 marksheet.
Age certificate.
Domicile certificate.
Caste certificate.
Income certificate of parents.
Village daughter certificate.
Samagra ID.
Current college code and branch code.
Mobile number and email id of the candidate.
Passport-size photograph of the candidate.
