Swaran Jayanti Ashray Yojana - Himachal Pradesh
Himachal Pradesh
Financial AssistanceHousingShelter
Details
The "Swaran Jayanti Ashray Yojana" was launched by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of Himachal Pradesh aims to provide financial assistance for the construction of houses who are permanent residents of Himachal Pradesh belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes. 

Benefits
Financial assistance of ₹1,50,000/- for construction of new house. 
Eligibility
The applicant should be a permanent resident of Himachal Pradesh. 
The applicant should belong to Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), or Other Backward Classes (OBC). 
The applicant's annual income does not exceed ₹50,000/-.
The applicant should not have his own house. 
The land in the applicant's name should be in the revenue record for the construction of the house.
Application Process
Offline
Application Process:
Step 1: The interested applicant can download the application form from the official website. 
Step 2: In the application form, fill in all the mandatory fields, and attach copies of all the mandatory documents (self-attest, if required).
Step 3: Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the Concerned District Welfare Officer/Tehsil Welfare Officer. 
Step 4: Request a receipt or acknowledgment from the Concerned District Welfare Officer/Tehsil Welfare Officer to whom the application has been submitted. Ensure that the receipt contains essential details such as the date and time of submission, and a unique identification number (if applicable).
Documents Required
Himachali Certificate (issued by the Executive Magistrate).
Caste Certificate.
Annual Income Certificate.
Tatima and Jamabandi of the land on which the house is proposed to be constructed and the proposal of the Gram Sabha.
Spot Report.
No Objection Certificate (NOC).
Proposal.
Photo of House (verified by Pradhan).
